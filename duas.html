📿 Duas by Emotion & Situation

Find Duas tailored to your emotional and spiritual needs.

😔 When Feeling Sad

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ
  • Transliteration: Allahumma inni a’udhu bika minal-hammi wal-huzn
  • Meaning: O Allah, I seek refuge in You from anxiety and sorrow.

😟 In Distress

  • Arabic: حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ
  • Meaning: Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs.

😇 Seeking Forgiveness

  • Arabic: أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللّٰهَ
  • Meaning: I seek forgiveness from Allah.

🌟 Gratitude

  • Arabic: الْـحَمْـدُ للهِ
  • Meaning: All praise is due to Allah.

😴 Before Sleeping

  • Arabic: بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا
  • Meaning: In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live.

❤️ Seeking Love & Mercy

  • Arabic: رَبِّ اجْعَلْنِي مُقِيمَ الصَّلَاةِ وَمِنْ ذُرِّيَّتِي
  • Meaning: My Lord, make me an establisher of prayer, and [many] from my descendants.

💼 Starting Something New

  • Arabic: رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي
  • Meaning: My Lord, expand for me my chest (give me confidence).
