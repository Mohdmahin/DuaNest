📿 Duas by Emotion & Situation

Find Duas tailored to your emotional and spiritual needs.

😔 When Feeling Sad

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ
  • Transliteration: Allahumma inni a’udhu bika minal-hammi wal-huzn
  • Meaning: O Allah, I seek refuge in You from anxiety and sorrow.

😟 In Distress

  • Arabic: حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ
  • Meaning: Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs.

😇 Seeking Forgiveness

  • Arabic: أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللّٰهَ
  • Meaning: I seek forgiveness from Allah.

🌟 Gratitude

  • Arabic: الْـحَمْـدُ للهِ
  • Meaning: All praise is due to Allah.

😴 Before Sleeping

  • Arabic: بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا
  • Meaning: In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live.

❤️ Seeking Love & Mercy

  • Arabic: رَبِّ اجْعَلْنِي مُقِيمَ الصَّلَاةِ وَمِنْ ذُرِّيَّتِي
  • Meaning: My Lord, make me an establisher of prayer, and [many] from my descendants.

💼 Starting Something New

  • Arabic: رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي
  • Meaning: My Lord, expand for me my chest (give me confidence).

🤲 Repentance

  • Arabic: رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَتُبْ عَلَيَّ إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيمُ
  • Meaning: My Lord, forgive me and accept my repentance. Indeed, You are the Accepter of repentance, the Merciful.

📖 Before Studying

  • Arabic: رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا
  • Meaning: My Lord, increase me in knowledge.

🚗 Traveling

  • Arabic: سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِينَ
  • Meaning: Glory to Him who has subjected this to us, and we could never have accomplished it [by ourselves].

😱 Fear or Anxiety

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخَوْفِ
  • Meaning: O Allah, I seek refuge in You from fear.

🌃 Waking Up

  • Arabic: الْـحَمْـدُ للهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا
  • Meaning: All praise is for Allah who gave us life after having taken it from us.

🌧️ During Rain

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ صَيِّبًا نَافِعًا
  • Meaning: O Allah, (bring) beneficial rain.

💊 When Sick

  • Arabic: أَذْهِبِ الْبَاسَ رَبَّ النَّاسِ اشْفِ أَنْتَ الشَّافِي
  • Meaning: Remove the harm, O Lord of the people, and heal, You are the Healer.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 For Parents

  • Arabic: رَّبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا
  • Meaning: My Lord, have mercy upon them as they brought me up [when I was] small.

😔 Loneliness

  • Arabic: لَا تَحْزَنْ إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَنَا
  • Meaning: Do not grieve; indeed Allah is with us.

🏠 Entering Home

  • Arabic: بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَلَجْنَا، وَبِسْمِ اللَّهِ خَرَجْنَا
  • Meaning: In the name of Allah we enter, and in the name of Allah we leave.

😔 After Losing Something

  • Arabic: إِنَّا لِلّهِ وَإِنَّـا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعونَ
  • Meaning: Indeed we belong to Allah, and indeed to Him we will return.

🍽️ After Eating

  • Arabic: الْـحَمْـدُ للهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا
  • Meaning: All praise is for Allah who fed us and gave us drink.

💪 In Difficulty

  • Arabic: إِنَّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا
  • Meaning: Indeed, with hardship comes ease.

🧠 Seeking Wisdom

  • Arabic: رَبِّ هَبْ لِي حُكْمًا وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ
  • Meaning: My Lord, grant me wisdom and join me with the righteous.

🏃 When in a Hurry

  • Arabic: رَبِّ يَسِّرْ وَلَا تُعَسِّرْ
  • Meaning: O my Lord, make it easy and do not make it difficult.

👀 Protection from Evil Eye

  • Arabic: أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّةِ مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْطَانٍ وَهَامَّةٍ
  • Meaning: I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from every devil and poisonous creature.

🛡️ Seeking Protection

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ احْفَظْنِي مِنْ بَيْنِ يَدَيَّ وَمِنْ خَلْفِي
  • Meaning: O Allah, protect me from in front of me and from behind me.

🕌 Entering Masjid

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ
  • Meaning: O Allah, open the doors of Your mercy for me.

👋 Leaving Masjid

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ
  • Meaning: O Allah, I ask You for Your favor.

💖 Expressing Love for Allah

  • Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُحِبُّكَ
  • Meaning: O Allah, I love You.
